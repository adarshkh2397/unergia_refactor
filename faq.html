When will the project start?

The project starts when the entire funding requirement is met. Investors can check the status any time through the online platform. Once the target is reached, the installation is done within a week.

What is my benefit?

Rooftop providers get cheaper electricity from a solar project, which is funded by the investors. This ensures they can avoid making the initial upfront investment.

Can I be investor and rooftop provider?

Yes, it's possible to be an investor if you are looking to diversify your investments. Contact Unergia Team for more details.

What is a PPA?

PPA stands for Power Purchasing Agreement. This is an agreement signed between Unergia and the Rooftop owner wherein Unergia installs the solar panels for zero upfront investment and takes a monthly fee from the Rooftop owner on the energy generated from the solar panel for the length of the contract.

Do I have to maintain the solar panels?

No, maintenance burden will be taken care of by Unergia for the length of the contract. The maintenance costs will be put in the funding target and to ensure that maintenance is done periodically.

What happens if the project is damaged or destroyed?

Unergia only partners with solar project retailers and manufacturers that can insure and warrant the projects for the period of contract. The insurance costs are included in the project costs that are to be funded.

What is net metering?

Net metering is a law that enables state governments to buy back the excess electricity produced by solar projects and the subsequent deductions are made on the electricity bills.

What is the length of the contract?

Contract are project specific. They shall last for 8-10 years.

Am I the owner of the panel?

Unergia is the owner of the panel. However after the end of the contract Unergia hands over the panels to the Rooftop providers.
